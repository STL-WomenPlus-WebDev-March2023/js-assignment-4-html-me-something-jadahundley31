A Spider Plant Named Charlotte

Spider plants are the easiest & safest plants to have in your home. I got Charlotte last year, around December, from a coworker. I was telling her how I wanted to get into planting, but did not know what plants to look for. As a gift, she gave me Charlotte. Her parents had a ton of spider plants throughout their house, so she was glad to give some away.

I water Charlotte every five days, or sometimes I would wait longer depending on how dry the soil is. I use to put her under a grow light for light source. However, she has gotten so big, she has to live on the floor next to my window. Every once in a while I would place her on my window sill to catch the sunshine. The website I use as a resource to take care of Charlotte can be found here.

Charlotte is growing like crazy & I can't wait to plant her into bigger vases!
